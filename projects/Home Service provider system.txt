🏠 Home Service Provider System
Overview

Home Service Provider System is a full-stack web application designed to connect customers with verified service professionals through a centralized online platform. The system simplifies the process of finding, booking, managing, and paying for household services such as electricians, plumbers, carpenters, cleaners, painters, appliance repair technicians, and other on-demand workers.

The platform acts as a digital marketplace where customers can browse services, schedule appointments, make secure online payments, and track service requests, while service providers can manage their profiles, bookings, earnings, and availability through dedicated dashboards.

The project was developed to address the challenges of traditional service booking methods, which often involve unreliable contacts, lack of transparency, manual scheduling, and payment difficulties.

Problem Statement

Finding trustworthy home service professionals can be difficult and time-consuming. Customers often face issues such as:

Lack of verified workers.
Unclear pricing.
Manual appointment scheduling.
No centralized booking platform.
Payment and transaction management difficulties.
Limited service tracking and customer support.

The Home Service Provider System solves these challenges by providing a secure and scalable platform that streamlines the entire service booking process from discovery to payment.

System Workflow
Step 1: User Registration

Customers create an account and complete profile setup.

Step 2: Service Discovery

Users browse available services and select a suitable service provider.

Step 3: Booking Creation

Customers choose:

Service category
Preferred date
Preferred time
Service location

The booking request is submitted to the selected worker.

Step 4: Booking Confirmation

The service provider receives the request and can:

Accept booking
Reject booking
Suggest schedule modifications
Step 5: Payment Processing

Users pay using their wallet balance or integrated payment methods.

Step 6: Service Completion

After completion:

Booking status is updated.
Payment is finalized.
Service records are stored.
Users can provide feedback.
