🏨 Hotel Management App
Overview

Hotel Management App is a cross-platform mobile application developed to simplify hotel room discovery, booking, and reservation management. The application provides customers with a seamless experience for browsing hotels, checking room availability, viewing room details, and making reservations directly from their mobile devices.

The system combines a Flutter-based mobile frontend with a PHP-powered backend and MySQL database, creating a scalable and efficient hotel booking platform. Through REST API integration and real-time data synchronization, users can access updated room information, pricing, and booking status instantly.

Problem Statement

Traditional hotel reservation systems often rely on manual processes or outdated web portals, making room booking inconvenient for customers and difficult to manage for hotel administrators.

Common challenges include:

Manual reservation management.
Limited room availability visibility.
Lack of mobile-friendly booking solutions.
Inefficient customer communication.
Difficulty managing bookings and room inventory.

The Hotel Management App addresses these issues by providing a centralized digital platform for hotel booking and management.

Application Workflow
Step 1: User Authentication

Users register or log in to access hotel booking services.

Step 2: Browse Hotels

Users view available hotels and room categories.

Step 3: View Room Details

Detailed room information, pricing, and amenities are displayed.

Step 4: Room Reservation

Users select check-in and check-out dates and submit booking requests.

Step 5: Booking Confirmation

The system validates availability and confirms the reservation.

Step 6: Booking Management

Users can view and manage their reservations through the application dashboard.
